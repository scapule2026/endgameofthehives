What is 15 Puzzle?

15 puzzle is a sliding puzzle made out of 15 numbered square pieces in a 4x4 grid, with one empty spot which allows for moving the pieces around. Instead of numbers, images are sometimes used as well.

Game Objective:

Place all 15 tiles in their correct (numerical) order

In this case, the puzzles is UNSOLVABLE and you should just try again. (sad, I know.)

LBL (Layer By Layer) Method

most commonly used by non-speedsolvers

1. Solve one row

~ Solve the two upper-left pieces

~ Place one of the last two pieces in

the correct layer but wrong position

~ Place the other piece on top

~ Solve the first piece using L or R

~ Solve the second piece by using U

2. Solve another row

3. Solve the two left-most pieces

similar to solving the last two pieces of one layer, but vertically

4. Solve the next two left-most pieces

5. Solve the remaining three pieces intuitively


The Fringe Method

used by speedsolvers (it's like LBL method but with SPEED)

1. Solve the top and left row to reduce the puzzle to an 8 puzzle

4x4 puzzle to a 3x3 puzzle

2. Repeat step number 1 again to reduce to a 3 puzzle

3x3 puzzle to a 2x2 puzzle

3. Solve the remaining three pieces intuitively
